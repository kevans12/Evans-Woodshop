Cutting Board/Rolling Pin Care:

We apply mineral oil to our cutting boards and rolling pins before sending them to you. To help maintain your cutting board or rolling pin, we suggest you continue to apply mineral oil or butcher block oil once a day for the first week, once a week for the first month, and then once a month after that, or as needed. To clean, simply wipe off or rinse with warm soapy water and dry immediately Never put these items in the dishwasher.
